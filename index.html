About me...


My name is Nathan Gee. In 2019 I left my former job of a retail Manager to pursue programming. Life was tough as I began my journey. I hunted for a job but had little jobs in-between to get by financially. I did roofing and I worked with a company writing standard operating procedures. I landed a job during Covid-19 in 2020. A company of the name Northwind Pharmaceuticals took me on for warehouse and I.T. work. I work there to this day.

On June 6th 2021 I was introduced to Eleven-Fifty Academy (EFA). They have a web development program that I was able to be a part of. The process of enrollment was smooth and I was a student 3 days later. At the point of writing this update I have finished 1/3rd of the program. I've come a long way of refining coding skills and logic. These skills cover HTML, CSS, JavaScript, UX/UI design, and even Agile Scrum. I am very pleased with my progress and I am ready to see how far journey takes me.
